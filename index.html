About Me

I am an Industrial Engineering student at Nnamdi Azikiwe University with a strong interest in system optimization, engineering innovation, and industrial development.

I was born and raised in Aba, a city widely known for its entrepreneurial spirit and manufacturing culture, which greatly influenced my passion for engineering and problem-solving. I come from Nenu Autonomous Community in Obingwa Local Government Area of Abia State, from the Onitcha clan.

Growing up in Aba exposed me to real-world business environments and practical production systems, which sparked my curiosity about how machines, processes, and industries work. My long-term goal is to contribute to industrial growth by building efficient systems, innovative technologies, and sustainable production solutions.
